Best Hiking Places in the World



Perito Moreno Glacier Trail, Argentina

Things to Know:

  1. How long is the hike?
    • 2.9 miles out and back
    • Options to trek on the ice or go close to the glacier by boat or kayak

  2. When is the best time to go?
    • November through June
    • Expect frigid winds and dress warm!

  3. Other information?
    • There are three different paths that offer different skill levels
    • Everyone has an opportunity to experience it!

Hooker Valley Track, New Zealand

Things to Know:

  1. How long is the hike?
    • Just over 6 miles
    • Elevation gain of 400 ft.

  2. When is the best time to go?
    • October through April
    • This is a popular area for camping, hiking, and walking

  3. Other information?
    • You can see views of New Zealand's highest peak, Aoraki Mt. Cook
    • Scenery is beautiful with unique geology, native trees, plentiful wildflowers, and fresh alpine air

Continental Divide Trail, New Mexico

Things to Know:

  1. How long is the hike?
    • 3.7 out and back hike
    • Elevation gain of 400 ft.

  2. When is the best time to go?
    • March through November
    • Pay attention to the snowpack!

  3. Other information?
    • Be careful of wildlife such as bears, mountain lions, and vultures
    • Very rocky trails so good footwear is recommended
